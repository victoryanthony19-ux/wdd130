Rivers State

Rivers State was created on May 27, 1967, from the former Eastern Region of Nigeria, named after its numerous rivers and located in the Niger Delta region. Its borders include the Atlantic Ocean to the south, Abia and Imo states to the north, Akwa Ibom State to the east, and Bayelsa and Delta states to the west. The state capital is Port Harcourt, a major commercial hub.
