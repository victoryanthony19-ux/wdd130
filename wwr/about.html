The Rapids Raftings

Rapids Raftings is a family owned business, created by my Mr. Anthony to give the most exciting experence and protection during your rafting process. The Marañón River is a major tributary of the Amazon River, flowing from the center of Perú in a northwest direction through the Andes to turn east in the north, then turning into the Ucayali River which joins to form the Amazon River.

History

Rapids is a family business created in the early 2000s by Mr. Anthony, with the aim of giving the best rafting experence you have ever had under quality guide and supervision. Rapids Rafting have had more than one hundred families, friends, companies and organizations since its opening. and you can be rest assured that your safety and fun experince is our top concern.
